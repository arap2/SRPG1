SARS-CoV-2 User Guide

This user guide aims to help visitors to the page to best access the wide variety of resources available here.

How to search the Protein database

Accession numbers found in this table can be directly entered into our PDB stuctures table search.

  1. Enter the Accesion number/Name/ID number of the protein sequence you're looking for in the 'Find a Protein Sequence' section
  2. A best match (if any) will be returned. Scan the table at the bottom to find relevant information
  3. Download the sequence by triple-clicking the information wanted
  4. Copy and paste relevant information into another one of our tables for more information

How to search the DNA database

  1. Enter the name or ID number of the DNA sequence you're looking for in the 'Find a DNA Sequence' section
  2. Scan the table at the bottom to find relevant information
  3. Download the sequence by triple-clicking the 'FASTA sequence' section

How to search the PDB database

Accession numbers found in this table can be directly entered into the PDB stuctures database: https://www.rcsb.org.

  1. Enter the name or ID number of the DNA sequence you're looking for in the 'Find a PDB structure' section
  2. Click 'Search' and scan the table at the bottom to find relevant information
  3. Once the desired accession number is found this can be entered directly on the PDB website to find your structure
